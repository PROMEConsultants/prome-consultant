Consultancy Services for Production of Driver Training Manual Handbook, a Companion Video/DVD, the Driving Test and Regulations to Control and Regulate Driving Schools, Driving Instructors and Examiners in Uganda

Project Information

Country: Uganda

Client Information

Name of Client: Ministry of Finance, Planning and Economic Development

Project Duration

Start Date: April 2007

Completion Date: October 2007

Approx. Value of Services: UGX 229,748,100/= (GoU)

Detailed Narrative Description of Project

The consultancy services involved the production of driver training manual handbook, a companion video/DVD, the driving tests, and regulations to control and regulate driving schools, driving instructors, and examiners. In particular, the following tasks were required:

  • Produce a driver manual and companion video/DVD. The content closely follows the new curriculum for training learner drivers.
  • Develop two practical and written driving tests that demand an appropriate level of knowledge and skill on the part of the learner to pass and obtain a license.
  • Advise the Ministry of Works, Housing, and Communications on how the driving tests, driving schools, and driving instructors shall be controlled and regulated.

Detailed Description of Actual Services Provided

  • Development and Production of Driver Training Manual and a Companion Video
  • Practical Driving Test
  • Introduction of Written Theory Test
  • Motorcycle and PSV/HGV Testing
  • Review of the Traffic and Road Safety Act, 1998, and drafting of regulations to control and regulate driving tests, driving schools, and driving instructors.
