Consultancy Services for Supervision of Impaputo-Goba Road Project

Country: Mozambique

Project Location within Country: Maputo Province

Name of Client: Government of Mozambique - Administração Nacional de Estradas

Financing Agency: IDA & Mozambique Government

Start Date: December 2002

Completion Date: July 2004

Approx. Value of Services €956,182.61

Names of Associated Firm(s) if any: BCEOM French Engineering Consultants

Narrative Description of Project:

Review of the detailed engineering design and construction supervision of the rehabilitation of a portion of Road EN-2 between Impaputo and Goba.

The project was divided into three different sections:

  • Section 1: 20 km in flat terrain, from the locality of Impaputo up to the Rio Umbeluzi, near Goba town.
  • Section 2: 2 km consisting of a bridge crossing the Umbeluzi river (360 m) and the road approaches.
  • Section 3: 8 km of mountainous terrain from the town of Goba up to the border with Swaziland.

Detailed Description of Actual Services Provided by Your Company:

The work of the consultant was particularly challenging because the design and construction started simultaneously, requiring fast results. Aerial photography surveys were conducted to facilitate highway design, study catchment areas, dimension smaller bridges, and improve the Umbeluzi river crossing.

Services Performed :

  1. Detailed Engineering Design of:
    • 22 km of section, including aerial photo survey, highway design, materials investigations, pavement design, and design of 6 bridges.
    • Structural design of a 10-span, 325 m bridge with prestressed beams of 32.6 m.
    • Highway design of 2 km of road approaches.
    • Highway design of the 8 km section to the border from Goba town.
  2. Supervision of Construction: Ensuring compliance with the design, quality control, and construction oversight.

The consultant appointed a team of engineers and experts to carry out the design work, as well as a team of supervisors to check the construction work.
