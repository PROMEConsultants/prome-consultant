Consultancy Services for the Construction Supervision of Konso-Yabello Road

Project Duration

2014 - 2019

Project Objectives

The project involved upgrading an existing gravel road to bitumen standards with the following major activities:

  • Improving the existing horizontal and vertical alignments and widening the road.
  • Construction of embankments.
  • Construction of drainage structures like cross culverts, box culverts, and bridges.
  • Pavement construction and asphalt works (wearing course bituminous layers).

Detailed Description of Actual Services Provided

  • General contract administration, measurement, and certification of works for payment.
  • Cost control and preparation of variation orders.
  • Monitoring public health issues on the project.
  • Environmental monitoring and supervision of civil works.
  • Guiding the setting out of works and controlling levels on the project.
  • Checking the quality of works and materials incorporated into the permanent works.
