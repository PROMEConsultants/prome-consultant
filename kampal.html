Consultancy Services for Development of Design Standards and Manuals for Construction and Maintenance of Urban Roads

Project Information

Country: Uganda

Project Location within Country: Kampala

Client Information

Name of Client: Ministry of Works and Transport

Project Duration

Start Date: 3rd November 2008

Completion Date: May 2009

Approx. Value of Services: UGX 116,400,000 (financed by GoU)

Detailed Narrative Description of Project

The consultancy services involved the following key activities:

  • Review of existing urban road works planning guidelines for road condition surveys and prioritizing works for the annual work plan.
  • Establishment of design parameters for roads in Urban Councils, including:
    • Road reserve for various categories of roads
    • Widths and cross-section details (horizontal and vertical) geometry for various categories of roads
    • Details of road-side parking, bus and taxi bays
    • Pedestrian walkways, raised and flush
    • Cycle tracks and PWD accesses
    • Kerb, flag edging details, and slabs
    • Stormwater drainage details (roadside gulley, gully trap, culvert headwall, wing wall, drop inlet details)
    • Street light pole details
    • Cross road ducts details
    • Road marking and road signs
    • Road junction details
  • Assessment of existing specifications for works, materials, and workmanship on current and past projects, with appropriate reviews.
  • Development of a standard Bills of Quantities for Urban Roads.

Detailed Description of Actual Services Provided

  • Developed a Planning Manual for Urban Roads Maintenance.
  • Developed a Design Manual for Urban Road Works.
  • Developed a Manual of Standard Specifications.
  • Developed a Standard Bill of Quantities for Urban Roads Construction and Maintenance.
  • Developed Guidelines on Construction or Maintenance Management.
  • Developed Reporting Manuals.
