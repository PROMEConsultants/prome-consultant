Consultancy Services for Supervision of Construction of Bridge Structures, Box Culverts, and Ferry Landing Sites Along Atiak-Moyo-Afoji Road

Country: Uganda

Project Location within Country: Atiak, Moyo

Name of Client: Uganda National Roads Authority

Start Date: May 2010

Completion Date: November 2014

Approx. Value of Services: UGX 2,200,000,000

Names of Associated Firm(s) if any: COMPTRAN Engineering

Narrative Description of Project:

The Atiak-Moyo-Afoji road is a gravel-surfaced road, approximately 104 km long, traversing rolling to hilly terrain through the districts of Amuru, Adjumani, and Moyo. Key project components included:

  • Ferry crossings at Laropi and Umi (approx. 66 km from Atiak)
  • Two bridges at Ayugi and Irei
  • Six box culverts located at Eyi-Ingewa, Surumu, Lower Cala, Upper Cala, Amua, and Ebikwa
  • Construction of ferry landing sites at Umi and Laropi

Detailed Description of Actual Services Provided by Your Company:

  • General contract administration, measurement, and certification of works for payment
  • Cost control and preparation of Variation Orders
  • Monitoring public health issues on the project
  • Environmental monitoring
  • Supervision of civil works
  • Guiding the setting out of works and controlling levels on the project
  • Checking the quality of works and materials to be incorporated into the permanent works
