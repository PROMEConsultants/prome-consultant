Physical Planning

Physical planning relates to design and managing of physical infrastructure including the zoning, land sub division and demarcating areas for development. The physical planning team carries out configurations of proposed infrastructure components to meet the future demand at a sufficient scale whist taking in to consideration technological changes. The team is well equipped to be able to Identify existing and future potential constraints as well as opportunities of different infrastructure investments. This is undertaken as a basis for further determination the overall planning principles and standards.
