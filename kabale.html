Feasibility Study on Strengthening and Improvement of the Northern Corridor

Country: Uganda

Project Location within Country: Kampala, Masaka, Mbarara, Kabale

Name of Client: Ministry of Works, Housing and Communications

Start Date: February 2000

Completion Date: October 2000

Approx. Value of Services: US$ 50,000

Names of Associated Firm(s) if any: GIBB Eastern Africa, Universal Engineering Services Ltd

Narrative Description of Project:

The project involved the improvement and strengthening of the Kampala-Masaka-Mbarara-Kabale-Katuna road.

Detailed Description of Actual Services Provided by Your Company:

  • Planning, execution, and input of traffic survey data into computer spreadsheets
  • Investigations of the properties of existing pavement layers using cored samples for laboratory analysis
  • Profile survey of the existing road surface using bump integrator equipment
  • Materials investigations to establish the availability of suitable materials for construction (borrow pit, stone quarry, and sand sources)
  • Preparation of the materials investigation report for submission to the client
