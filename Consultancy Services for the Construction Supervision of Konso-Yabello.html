Consultancy Services for the Construction Supervision of Konso-Yabello Road

✅ Project Duration:

2014 - 2019

✅ Project Objectives:

The project objectives include but are not limited to the following:

• To improve access to goods and passenger transport services and reduce transport costs along the route.

• To improve access to social and economic development opportunities along the route by providing high-capacity infrastructure.

• To ensure no roadside communities become worse off as a result of the road construction works.

✅ Project Description:

The project involved upgrading an existing gravel road to bitumen standards with major activities including:

• Improving the existing horizontal and vertical alignments, widening the road, and constructing embankments.

• Constructing drainage structures such as cross culverts, box culverts, and bridges.

• Pavement construction and asphalt works, including wearing course bituminous layers.

✅ Detailed Description of Actual Services Provided:

• General contract administration, measurement, and certification of works for payment, cost control, and preparation of variation orders.

• Monitoring public health issues on the project.

• Environmental monitoring and supervision of civil works.

• Guiding the setting out of works and controlling levels on the project, ensuring quality control of materials used in permanent works.
