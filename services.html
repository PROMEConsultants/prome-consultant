Services

PROME Consultants Limited is a knowledge-based firm engaged in the provision of Project Management and Engineering Consultancy Services and Solutions particularly in the Built Environment. As an ISO Certified firm, PROME Consultants has strong expertise in the provision of reliable knowledge based engineering and project management services focusing on key engineering areas of civil engineering and building, electrical, mechanical/industrial, environmental, transportation and energy using state-of-the-art competencies, and in full compliance of company core values on one hand and the national, regional and international standards on the other.

PROME Consultants has accorded special focus on the infrastructure development because without the needed infrastructure, society is denied the capacity to sustain itself socially and/or economically.

PROME Consultants offers comprehensive consultancy project/program management and engineering services focused on addressing of the customers needs right from customer needs identification and assessment to product delivery, operation and maintenance.
