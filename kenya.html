Detailed Narrative Description of Project

The project involved the feasibility study for a petroleum products pipeline from Eldoret to Kampala. The following tasks were carried out:

  • Task review and update of the existing study
  • Detailed comparative cost/benefit analysis of the competing transport modes between Mombasa and Kampala over a reasonable time frame
  • Risk sensitivity analysis using different scenarios
  • Study of different options of ownership and management structure for the pipeline extension as well as ownership and management of the existing structure
  • Consideration of investment requirements necessary to sustain the integrity of the Mombasa â€“ Eldoret pipeline
  • Review of different private investment options available under different ownership and management structures and recommendation of the most feasible plan
  • Preparation of a bid document that covers public invitation to potential investors for expression of interest, project information memorandum, and model agreement between the investor and the governments of Kenya and Uganda
