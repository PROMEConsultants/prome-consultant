Who we are

PROME Consultants Limited is a knowledge-based firm engaged in the provision of Project Management and Engineering Consultancy Services and Solutions, particularly in the Built Environment.

PROME's overall value proposition is enshrined in its Mission Statement, which is

“To deliver innovative and sustainable quality Engineering and Technologically trending Digital solutions that more than satisfy customer needs as well as meeting the Company's values and objectives.”

Prome therefore delivers sustainable Innovative, Quality, Economical, Cost Effective, Reliable and Relevant Engineering and Technical and Digital Solutions and Project Management Services. PROME partners with its clientele to create solutions that satisfy the clientele's objectives.

Corporate Planning and Strategy Division (CPSD)

Corporate Planning and Strategy Division offers strategic directional planning to the company in tandem with the global Trending Applicable Digital Technical Solutions and ensures appropriate collaborative external professional linkages and visibility in this regard. In addition, the Division maintains oversight into the workings of each of the company divisions to ensure delivery of integrated, holistic and innovative solutions to more than meet our Client's needs.

Project Management and Business Development Division

PROME’s Project Management and Business Development Division guarantees compliance of all company operations and outputs with International Quality management procedures and Client Specifications through exhaustive quality checks, and reviews as well as internal audits. The Division maintains the company’s MIS and is the custody of all the company’s technical records. In this regard, the PMBDO is responsible for the preparation of competitive, attractive and realistic technical proposals of the Company while ensuring maximum technical and financial benefit to the Client.

Further more, the division contracts out the Company's technical Project Management Services to the construction industry, which services include, but are not limited to, the following: (i) Value Engineering & Optimization; (ii) Claims Management; (iii) Cadastral Surveys & Valuations, (iv) Resettlement Action Planning, (v) Social/Environmental Studies, and (vi) Economic Studies

Planning and Engineering Division (PED)

PROME’s Planning and Engineering Division is the Company’s set-up that undertakes the planning, design and engineering of the infrastructure technological solutions of the built environment which are technically appropriate, economically justifiable, financially affordable, socially acceptable, environmentally responsible and institutionally manageable. These solutions include the following: Physical & Structural Planning; Transportation & Highways Engineering; and Water & Sanitation Engineering The Division undertakes the planning design and engineering functions by use of state of the art and innovative technological applications and software for its technical surveys, technical engineering design, architecture, and space technologies.

To ensure that the solutions more than meet our client's needs, the Division undertakes (i) Value Engineering & Optimization; (ii) Cadastral Surveys & Valuations, (iii) Resettlement Action Planning, (iv) Social/Environmental Studies, and (v) Economic Studies, as may be Relevant to the Executed Design and Engineering Solutions.

Our Engineering designs stand out for their technical excellence. All of our engineers have a strong understanding of multiple design disciplines and believe in the importance of working closely with all teams on a project. This allows everyone to fully understand each other’s requirements and optimizes the design outcome.

Project Delivery Management Division (PDMD)

PROME’s project Delivery Management Division is involved in the evolving projects delivery mechanisms and trends that include: PPP, EPC, DB EPC+F & BOOT Engagements. In this regard, the Division ensures efficient and effective delivery of fit for purpose, quality, and on-time Construction Management of the construction works within available budget. Our engineers have worked on large, global scale projects and have witnessed first-hand how quickly project scopes and costs can increase because of unforeseen complexities, unexpected ground conditions or lack of experience.
