Consultancy Services for the Supervision of Maintenance Works on Selected National Roads, Package 9

Country: Uganda

Project Location within Country: Rakai, Mbarara, Isingiro, Bushenyi

Name of Client: Uganda National Roads Authority

No. of Staff: 5

Start Date: September 2010

Completion Date: April 2011

Approx. Value of Services: UGX 382,300,000/=

Narrative Description of Project:

Periodic maintenance (regravelling) of four selected national roads (totaling 107Km) in Central Uganda. The roads included:

  • Lusalira-Kasambya-Nkonge Road (39Km)
  • Ngoma-Bulyamusenyu Road (32Km)
  • Wobulenzi-Bamunanika-Zirobwe Road (24Km)
  • Kalule-Bamunanika Road (12Km)

Detailed Description of Actual Services Provided by Your Company:

  • General contract administration, measurement, and certification of works for payment
  • Cost control and preparation of Variation Orders
  • Monitoring public health issues on the project
  • Environmental monitoring
  • Supervision of civil works
  • Guiding the setting out of works and controlling levels on the project
  • Checking the quality of works and materials to be incorporated into the permanent works
