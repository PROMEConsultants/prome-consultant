Detailed Narrative Description of Project

The project involved the feasibility study and design of the Kampala Northern Bypass, which included the following tasks:

  • Review and necessary revisions of traffic forecasts
  • Assessment of the relevance of the proposed by-pass to address identified problems in the economic and social sectors
  • Detailed analysis of the technical, economic, financial, institutional, management, environmental, and socio-cultural feasibility of the project
  • Assessment of the sustainability of the project results
  • Development of a detailed plan specifying project objectives, results, activities, and required resources
  • Identification of properties to be expropriated
  • Identification of public buildings to be relocated, along with technical design and tender documents
  • Detailed engineering design, technical specifications, and tender documents for physical works and supervision
  • Evaluation of tenders from contractors responding to the tender documents
