Detailed Narrative Description of Project:

✅ Consultancy services for carrying out topographical surveys, geotechnical, and geo-electrical surveys along the new Kampala-Entebbe Expressway

Detailed Description of Actual Services Provided by Our Company:

✅ Site investigations and laboratory tests to define parameters required for geotechnical characterization of soils in the pipeline route, including boreholes with continuous soil recovery

✅ Identification tests such as particle size analysis and consistency limits on soil fraction passing the sieve

✅ Geotechnical characterization of foundation soils based on site investigations and laboratory test results

✅ Geotechnical survey for all bridge location sites, swamp crossings, and deep cuts, including boreholes with continuous soil recovery and SPT testing
