PROME CONSULTANTS LIMITED’s Corporate Strategy

is to be a diversified, innovative company. We will pursue an agile, competitive, and diversified business strategy focused on human, financial, and technological capabilities, while considering the prevailing market conditions, forecasted opportunities, and societal needs. This strategy will also emphasize competitiveness and collaboration within the local and international engineering industry.

Objectives

The PROME Strategic objectives for the period from July 1st, 2019, to December 31st, 2025, include:

To ensure constant innovation and agility

in light of today’s rapidly changing market and customer satisfaction needs. This includes staying focused on delivering state-of-the-art engineering solutions by driving agile digital transformation, focusing on digitalized data, automation of processes, AI, and further diversifications.

To meet and exceed client expectations

and satisfaction, even in the most complex situations. This means ensuring the timely delivery of quality solutions, maintaining successful customer relations, and anticipating the future needs of customers. It is through these strategies that we aim to build long-term customer loyalty.

To continuously grow staff competence

through ongoing development of knowledge, professionalism, teamwork, and skill enhancement.

To enhance professional efficiency, accountability, and integrity

among all staff members, fostering an environment of trust and respect.

To ensure sustained corporate profitability and growth

by focusing on sustained profitability, optimal resource utilization, and reducing avoidable operational costs.
