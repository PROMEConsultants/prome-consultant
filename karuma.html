Resealing and Rehabilitation of Kafu – Karuma Road

Country: Uganda

Project Location within Country: Northern Uganda

Name of Client: Ministry of Works, Housing and Communications

Start Date: October 1999

Completion Date: November 1999

Financing Agency: World Bank

Names of Associated Firm(s) if any: RENARDET SA (Switzerland)

Narrative Description of Project:

The project involved the rehabilitation and resealing of the existing 92 km two-lane single carriageway Class I bitumen road from Kafu to Karuma. The works included minor pothole repair, resealing of approximately 69 km, and reconstruction of approximately 23 km of road.

Detailed Description of Actual Services Provided by Your Company:

  • Deflection survey using Benkelman beam
  • DCP (Dynamic Cone Penetrometer) survey
  • Preparation of pavement evaluation report
  • Visual condition survey
  • Test pits for density tests and sampling
  • Material survey of borrow pit areas
  • Laboratory testing
  • Analysis of results from field investigation/testing
