Geological Surveys

At PROME, we provide a complete service from materials sampling, in-situ testing and analysis through to the preparation of Interpretative Reports. Our team of engineers carries out desk studies, plans and designs ground investigations, and produces Geotechnical Interpretative Reports. We have experience across various projects, including buildings, roads and bridges, oil and gas facilities, hydropower projects, and wastewater treatment facilities.

The effects of ground behavior on the built and natural environment can be far-reaching. Our geotechnical department has conducted extensive work, developing an enriched GIS database of ground investigations in the region, which we can use during the planning stages of projects.

To complement this, PROME Consultants offers geotechnical advice services from project inception through to construction. We also provide expert witness services, advising clients during legal proceedings when failures or distress to structures are suspected to arise from geotechnical issues.
