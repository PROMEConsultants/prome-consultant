Framework contract for the provision of road engineering consultancy services for upgrading of roads in Kampala Capital City Authority for Mutundwe RD, Weraga RD,Wasoso RD under lot 2

Project Duration:

January 2014 - September 2016

Project Description

The scope of works under the project largely involve upgrading of existing road from gravel to bitumen standards with the following major activities:
  • Improving of the existing horizontal and vertical alignments and Widening of the existing road
  • Construction of embankments
  • Construction of drainage structures like cross culverts Pavement
  • Construction and Asphalt Works/Wearing course bituminous layers Ancillary Works.

    • Detailed Description of Actual Services Provided by your Company:

  • General contract administration, measurement and certification of works for payment, cost control and preparation of Variation Orders
  • Monitoring the Public health issues on the project
  • Environmental Monitoring
  • Supervision of the Civil works
  • Guiding the setting out of works and controlling levels on the project
  • Checking the quality of works and materials to be incorporated in the permanent works
