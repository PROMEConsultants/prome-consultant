Project Management

With our regional presence, we bring together experienced professionals from the region and across the globe to provide project and contract management services to our clients.

Infrastructure and construction projects are habitually complex, mainly in terms of scope and priorities. Over the past two decades, PROME has reliably delivered construction projects proficiently, even in the most challenging locations and under difficult institutional circumstances.

With the intention of delivering the desired outcome, our projects are administered with utmost transparency by engaging all relevant stakeholders. PROME is tailored to mitigate risks associated with running projects and capitalizes on opportunities to guarantee value for money.

PROME, with its innovative culture and extensive experience, has put in place processes that enable the development of your delivery strategy and management plan, ensuring its practical implementation. We guide you judiciously from the initial stages through to the final project handover.

To ensure projects are delivered on time and within budget, we leverage strong partnerships with our clients, supporting their best interests while optimizing value for money during project delivery.

The services we offer to our clients include:

  • Bid document preparation services for construction companies
  • Project contract management and support
  • Development of customized project management information systems and quality assurance systems for effective, cost-efficient, and timely project implementation
  • Expert services for construction claims mitigation, avoidance, and management
  • Economic studies, social and environmental impact assessments, and land acquisition services
  • Project and program development, documentation, and construction supervision
  • Development of design standards and manuals for construction
