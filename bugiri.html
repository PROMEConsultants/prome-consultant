Supervision of the Re-Construction of Jinja-Bugiri Road

Country: Uganda

Project Location within Country: Eastern Region

Name of Client: Ministry of Works, Housing, and Communications/Road Agency Formation Unit

Funded by: European Union (EU)

Start Date: 19th January 2004

Completion Date: December 2010

Approx. Value of Services / Financing Agency: UGshs 3,351,544,867

Names of Associated Firm(s) if any: BCEOM French Engineering Consultants

Narrative Description of Project:

The project involves the reconstruction and strengthening of the entire 76 km of Jinja-Bugiri road. The road is to be widened in some sections, and a bypass will be constructed at Magamaga. The pavement is to be either an overlay surfacing or involve recycling and stabilizing the existing base layer, followed by crushed stone base, prime coat, DBM, and Asphalt Concrete in certain sections.

Detailed Description of Actual Services Provided by Your Company:

  • General contract administration, measurement, and certification of works for payment, cost control, and preparation of Variation Orders
  • Monitoring public health issues on the project
  • Environmental monitoring
  • Supervision of civil works
  • Guiding the setting out of works and controlling levels on the project
  • Checking the quality of works and materials to be incorporated in the permanent works
